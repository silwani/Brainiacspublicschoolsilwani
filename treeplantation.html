Tree Plantation Drive 2023

Brainiacs Public School's Green Initiative

About the Event

Our annual Tree Plantation Drive was held on March 12, 2023, in collaboration with the local environmental department. Over 150 students, teachers, and community members participated in this green initiative to combat climate change and promote environmental awareness.

The event featured educational workshops about sustainable practices, the importance of urban greenery, and hands-on planting activities. Each student was responsible for planting and adopting a tree, ensuring its growth and care throughout the year.

Event Highlights

  • Planted over 200 native tree species
  • Educational workshops on environmental conservation
  • Participation certificates awarded to all students
  • Community engagement with local environmentalists
  • Ongoing tree monitoring program established

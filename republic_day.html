Celebrating Our Constitutional Values

The Republic Day celebration at Brainiacs Public School Silwani was a grand affair filled with patriotic fervor. The event began with the hoisting of the national flag, followed by the singing of the national anthem that echoed through the campus.

Our students presented a spectacular cultural program showcasing India's rich diversity through dances, songs, and skits. The march past by the school houses displayed discipline and unity, reminding us of our armed forces' valor.

This year's celebration focused on educating students about their constitutional rights and duties. Special assemblies were conducted where teachers explained the significance of the Preamble and fundamental rights.

We reaffirm our commitment to nurturing responsible citizens who will uphold the values of justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity enshrined in our Constitution.

Our Mission for a Cleaner India

The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan at Brainiacs Public School Silwani is not just a campaign but a movement towards creating responsible citizens who value cleanliness and hygiene.

Our students actively participate in weekly cleanliness drives, where they clean classrooms, playgrounds, and surrounding areas. We emphasize the importance of waste segregation and have implemented recycling bins across the campus.

Through awareness programs and workshops, we educate our students about the harmful effects of plastic and the importance of sustainable living. Our school has completely banned single-use plastics and promotes the use of eco-friendly alternatives.

We believe that cleanliness starts with each individual, and through our collective efforts, we can make India cleaner and greener.
